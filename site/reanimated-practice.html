REANIMATED

Charlie has enrolled at Miskatonic University ready to make new friends and party. His flatmate, a mad scientist, has other ideas.

Reanimated is a short film directed by Tom Byrne based on the short story by HP Lovecraft. It was funded through a successful Kickstarter campaign, which can be viewed here. The film is currently in post-production.

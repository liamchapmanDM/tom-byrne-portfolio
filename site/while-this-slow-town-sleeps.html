WHILE THIS SLOW TOWN SLEEPS

We shot this using the Sony RX0 camera, as part of a competition. The RX0 is a brilliantly small camera - that size opened up a lot of possibilities for the story we were telling over one night in London. I’ve known Eugene for a few years now, and loved his photography from the start. We’d talked about collaboration; when this challenge came up, it felt like the right thing to do.
